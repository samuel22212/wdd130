A bit about me:

I live in sunny Salt Lake city, UT with my wife, Audrey, and pet husky. For the past 3 years I've developed games and studied while working at a variety of call centers. These terrible customer experiences, and a lifetime full of hatred from competitive gaming, drove me to wantr to create the most brutal, painful, and challenging video games I could imagine to make other suffer as I have. Of course my creative counterpart, Audrey, despises difficult video games which allows for reasonable compromise in their design. I truly believe if it wasn't for her nobody would be able to stand any of the things I've made or done so far. I always found the best challenges are overcome with critical thinking so I've made it a personal philosophy of mine to make any challenge in the games I make a mental one first and an ability challenge second. I feel this also makes the games and puzzles I design more accessible as well.

Of course, there is a lot more to what I do than just creating video games. I enjoy problem solving and buildin which is what drove me to also learn to create websites such as the one you are on now. I have a knack for getting things to work eventually. Whether it is a puzzle, game, or error message, with enough time and patience I am able to snake my way through it and get it done. This talent is something I am proud of and I make good use of it in my daily life in the form of solving household issues to finding issues with the way we do things at my place of work. I would almost describe it as a kind of productive laziness where my mind wants to solve as many problems as possible using the least amount of brain power or effort.

I love meeting and working with people. If you have any questions or want to reach out to me about a project please use my business email samuelporter219@gmail.com
